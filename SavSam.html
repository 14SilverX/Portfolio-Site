Savage Sam

Portfolio | About Me | Contact
Specifications
  • Language: C++
  • Engine: Unreal 4
  • Platform: Windows

Descrption
  • You're late again! You play as Savage Sam in an 80's style action sidescroller after he missed the bus to school! Avoid obstacles and collect power ups as you chase the bus down the street. Jump on the bus to make it school and receive a grade for the day. The better your grade the more allowance money you get. Buy and upgrade items from the school black market to improve your run. Make sure to make it to school on time!

Role
  • I came up with the original idea for this game as an arcade style game about a kid who wakes up late for school. I also programed all screens that are not part of the gameplay; the main menu, the report card and the shop. All programing was done using Unreal Blueprints. I also composed the music for the game using Ableton Lite.
